Charis Stamouli

I am a PhD student in the GRASP Lab at University of Pennsylvania (UPenn), where I work under the supervision of Professor George J. Pappas. My research interests lie in the intersection of learning, dynamics and control.

Before joining UPenn, I worked as an undergraduate research assistant in the Control Systems Laboratory at National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), where I was advised by Professor Kostas J. Kyriakopoulos. I received my diploma (MEng, five-year degree) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from NTUA.
